Advanced Google Search Tool

Select a prefix to see its use and example.
You can combine multiple prefixes for more specific searches. For example:
  • site:wikipedia.org intitle:"Climate Change" - Finds pages on Wikipedia with "Climate Change" in the title.
  • filetype:pdf intext:"data analysis" - Searches for PDF files that contain "data analysis" in their text.
  • related:example.com site:edu - Finds educational sites related to example.com.
